About Us - Welcome to Garage Sale!

At Garage Sale, we believe every item has a story waiting to be discovered. Born out of a love for hidden gems and sustainable shopping, our platform serves as a digital crossroads for Vienna's vibrant flea market scene. Whether you're a seasoned collector, a sustainable shopper, or someone looking to declutter, Garage Sale is your go-to destination.


Our Mission and Vision

Mission: To enrich the Vienna community by making it easy for anyone to participate in the flea market experience, fostering sustainability, and encouraging local commerce.

Vision: To create a sustainable future where every item gets a second chance at love. We dream of a world where Garage Sale is not just a platform but a movement towards mindful consumption and community solidarity.
